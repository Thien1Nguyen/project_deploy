About Us

Every family has their own traditional dishes passed down from generation to generation. That's why it's so important to have a family-owned and operated restaurant where you can enjoy the taste of authentic Vietnamese cuisine. At Pho Gia Long, you can find traditional Vietnamese food that is both delicious and healthy. We believe that cooking with love makes all the difference and we give our customers nothing less than our best! We take pride in our traditional Vietnamese cuisine that is served with fresh ingredients and vegetarian and gluten free options. Our delicious menu will leave you feeling satisfied, whether you are coming into our restaurant or ordering online. We offer online ordering services or dine-in at our cozy location on Katella.
